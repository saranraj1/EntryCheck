# Pilot Results Snippet

Across ten seeds, the fitted logistic model achieved mean ROC AUC 0.878 (SD 0.015) and recovered the generating coefficient direction with cosine similarity 0.998 (SD 0.001). At 200 evaluated explanations per seed, exact linear attributions achieved mean deletion fidelity 1.764, compared with 0.753 for the randomized negative control. Mean Top-3 stability was 0.957 for exact attributions and 0.259 for the negative control. These values validate the expected directional behavior of the pilot metrics but must not be interpreted as evidence about SHAP, LIME, or real-world deployments.
